Chanel · 100ml · Eau de Parfum

Experience refined luxury where rare Cambodian Oud meets warm Baltic Amber. It opens with a touch of saffron and cardamom, evolving into a rich heart of resins and Bulgarian rose. This fragrance captures the essence of the Orient, offering depth, sophistication, and a memorable presence in any setting.

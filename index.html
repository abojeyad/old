Bleu de Chanel EDP
Chanel · 100ml · Eau de Parfum

Experience refined luxury where rare Cambodian Oud meets warm Baltic Amber. It opens with a touch of saffron and cardamom, evolving into a rich heart of resins and Bulgarian rose. The base of white musk, patchouli, and sandalwood creates a lasting, elegant trail for over 12 hours. This fragrance captures the essence of the Orient, offering depth, sophistication, and a memorable presence in any setting.

Help & FAQ
How does Yourdis track prices? +
Yourdis scans major Saudi fragrance stores multiple times daily and logs every price change automatically. You see the most up-to-date price the moment it changes.
When will I be notified of a price drop? +
The moment a tracked fragrance drops to or below your set target price, you receive a push notification and an email alert instantly.
Which stores does Yourdis cover? +
We currently cover Golden Scent, Asheq Al-Otor, Najd Al-Otheyah, V Perfume, Deraah, Al-Sultan Perfumes and more. New stores are added regularly.
Is Yourdis free to use? +
Yes — Yourdis is completely free. You can track unlimited fragrances, set alerts, and compare prices at no cost.
How do I delete my account? +
You can request account deletion by contacting support@yourdis.sa. Your data will be permanently removed within 7 days.
